Sales and Revenue Dashboard

Objective: To create an interactive Power BI dashboard to monitor regional sales and revenue performance for a mock retail business.

Methods:

Integrated data from multiple sources, including SQL databases and Excel files, to provide a unified view of sales performance across regions.

Used Power Query to clean and transform the data for analysis, ensuring accuracy and consistency.

Designed KPIs like total revenue, profit margins, and sales growth rates using DAX functions in Power BI.

Deliverables:

Developed a dynamic dashboard with filters for time periods, regions, and product categories, enabling granular insights for decision-making.

Provided insights on underperforming regions and suggested targeted interventions to improve sales.

Outcome: Gained proficiency in Power BI, SQL, and DAX, while showcasing data visualization skills for business applications.
