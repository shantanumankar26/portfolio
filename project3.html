Retail Customer Segmentation

Objective: To analyze customer purchasing behavior and segment them into distinct groups for personalized marketing strategies.

Methods:

Utilized a retail dataset with over 10,000 customer records. Cleaned and processed the data using Python (Pandas and NumPy).

Performed exploratory data analysis (EDA) to identify key attributes influencing customer segmentation, such as frequency of purchases and average transaction value.

Applied K-means clustering in Python (scikit-learn) to group customers into segments based on their behavior.

Deliverables:

Developed a Power BI dashboard showcasing the segmentation results and key metrics for each customer group.

Recommended targeted marketing campaigns for each segment, such as discounts for high-value customers and retention strategies for infrequent shoppers.

Outcome:

Strengthened Python, machine learning, and visualization skills while providing practical business solutions.
