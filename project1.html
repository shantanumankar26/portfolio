Generation-Y Perception on Cloud Kitchens as a Disruptive Innovation

Objective: To explore how Generation-Y perceives cloud kitchens as a disruptive innovation in the food industry and to identify factors influencing adoption and satisfaction.

Methods:

Designed a survey to gather insights from 300+ participants, focusing on factors like convenience, affordability, and quality.

Used Python libraries like Pandas, NumPy, and Matplotlib to clean, analyze, and visualize the collected data.

Conducted hypothesis testing and regression analysis to identify statistically significant predictors of satisfaction and adoption.

Deliverables:

Developed a comprehensive Power BI dashboard to present findings, visualizing trends and key insights from the research.

Presented actionable recommendations to improve marketing strategies for cloud kitchens, with an emphasis on customer retention and competitive differentiation.

Outcome:

Demonstrated advanced research, data analysis, and visualization skills, earning a Merit for the dissertation.
